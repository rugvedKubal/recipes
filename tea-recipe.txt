Ingredients:
  - 3 table spoons of tea powder
  - 5 table spoons of sugar
  - 1/4 inch ginger
  - 2 cups of water
  - 1 cup milk

Directions:
  1. Turn on the stove with high flame.
  2. Add water and milk to a bowl and keep it on stove.
  3. Crush the ginger and add it to bowl; let it simmer for a wile.
  4. Add tea powder.
  5. Add sugar.
  6. Wait until the tea is rolling boil.
  7. Delicious masala tea is ready!
